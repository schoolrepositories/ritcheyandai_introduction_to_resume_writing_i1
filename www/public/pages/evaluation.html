A resume is a ____ of a person's skills, experience, and education.


summary

work history

education

skills

contact information

Applicant

scan

ATS

PDF

DOCX

PDF

summary

chronological

functional

functional

one

personal information

action-oriented

readability

tailoring

relevant

certifications

images
